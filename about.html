About Us

Home About Us

Our Source of animals

We mainly get our dogs from the street, including ones with diseases or experienced accidents. Some of them are sent by others and some of them give childbirth in the shelter. We have 146 animals in total.

Our Purpose

Our purpose is to safe homeless cats and dogs, to offer them some food, some water and a safe place to live.

Facilities

Our facilities are a bit crude, such as cages built with wooden poles, because we just moved to here.

Staffs

We have 2 volunteers with 15 years of working experiences in the field of animal conservation form 2004-2019.We repeat our daily chores, such as cleaning kennel, getting water, cooking and taking care of the sicks. Our staffs: 高秀丽 (Gao Xiu Li), 高霞 (gao xia)
